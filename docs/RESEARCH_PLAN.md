# Research and Remediation Plan for Missing Components and Issues

### Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive research and remediation plan for 13 missing components and issues in the CloudWS-bootc project. The plan is organized by phase, and includes milestones, deliverables, research tasks, upstream references, and a Kanban-style tracking matrix.

---

## Phases

### Phase 1: Identification
- **Milestone:** Complete identification of all missing components.
- **Deliverables:** Detailed list of missing components.
- **Research Tasks:**  
  1. Review existing documentation.  
  2. Consult with the team for inputs.  

### Phase 2: Research
- **Milestone:** Conduct research for each missing component.
- **Deliverables:** Research findings documents for each component.
- **Research Tasks:**  
  1. Identify upstream references.  
  2. Analyze solutions from similar projects.

### Phase 3: Remediation
- **Milestone:** Remediate issues based on research findings.
- **Deliverables:** Implemented solutions for all components.
- **Research Tasks:**  
  1. Develop initial solutions.  
  2. Test and validate components.
